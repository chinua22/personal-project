Recipe for Jollof Rice

My Favourite Food

Cook your way up to chef.

  • 3 fresh pepper
  • 6 chilli pepper (tatashe)
  • 3 sweet pepper (shombo)
  • 5 bulbs of onions
  • 3 garlic cloves anda root of ginger
  • 3Tbsps tomatoe paste
  • 1/2 tbsp curry powder and 1 tbsp thyme
  • 1/2 Nutmeg and 1/2 tbsp crayfish
  • 3cups chicken stock and 5 bay leaves
  • 5 seasoning cubes and 1 tbsp grounded pepper
  • 5cups of rice and 1/2 cup veg.oil
  • salt to taste
  • 1kilo Chicken

Utensils needed

  • Aluminium Steel Pot
  • Wooden spatula
  • Water
  • Foil paper 2parts
  • Gas cooker
    wash the rice about 6 to 7 times
    blend the sweet pepper, bell pepper, ginger and garlic with a little quantity of water
    chop 2 bulbs onion and add to the pre heated oil cook for 45secs
    add tomatoes paste into the onion and fry for 5 to 8 mins keep stirring to avoid burning
    pour the blended sauce into the paste and cook for 7mins
    Add stock into the sauce
    add the following
      thyme and curry then stir
      seasoning, crayfish and grounded pepper
      Nutmeg and salt to taste then stir
    Add rice into the sauce then enough water to cook, then stirr
    Use the foil to cover the foil helps the steam cook the rice
    Add bay leaves then cover for 10-15 mins
    remove bay leaves and stirr
    Increase the heat to burn for a whilethis gives it the party taste
